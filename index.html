Features

nature

Handcrafted, home-made wooden digeridoos

Our digeridoos are 100% made of high quality eucalyptus wood sourced from Australia's Mountain Ash region. We do not deal with PVC.

local_shipping

Fast Shipping

You can choose from 3 ways of delivery options. We oparate with industry leader shipping companies. For our Premium plus customers and for orders above 150EUR we charge no shipping fees.

Quality Assurance

Our instruments are quality inspected by the Digeridoo Foundation, the leading organization in the industry.
